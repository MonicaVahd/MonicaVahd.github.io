## About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the [University of Ottawa](https://www2.uottawa.ca/en), conducting research at the [MCRLab](https://mcr.uottawa.ca) under the supervision of **Professor Abdulmotaleb El Saddik**. My dissertation, ***FAIR: A Flexible, Agentic, Intelligent, Real-Time Coach for Multimodal Posture Correction***, develops a novel agentic AI system that integrates LLM-driven decision-making, multi-agent orchestration (CrewAI, Agent-to-Agent protocol), and reinforcement learning for real-time, personalized fitness coaching.

My research sits at the intersection of **Agentic AI**, **Computer Vision**, **Multimodal Digital Twins**, and **Reinforcement Learning** — building systems that perceive, reason, and act in real-time embodied environments.

Before my Ph.D., I spent nine years as a Software Developer at Tamin ICT & Management Consultancy, designing 40+ enterprise-grade applications for Iran's insurance and financial sectors.
